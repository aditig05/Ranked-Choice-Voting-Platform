How Ranked Choice Voting Works

1

Rank Your Choices

Instead of choosing just one candidate, rank them in order of your preference.

2

Automatic Runoffs

If no candidate gets a majority, the last-place candidate is eliminated and votes are redistributed.

3

Majority Winner

The process continues until one candidate receives more than 50% of the vote.

Why Ranked Choice Voting?

More Choice

Vote for your favorite candidate without worrying about "wasting" your vote.

Better Representation

Winners have broader support from the electorate.

Positive Campaigns

Candidates have an incentive to appeal to a broader range of voters.
